About Us

Welcome to the Academy of Foreign Language! We are a virtual and in-person learning environment that specializes in teaching lesser known alternative languages. We are currently offering beginner, intermediate, and expert classes in Zulu, Swahili, Croatian, Tahitian, Finnish, and Romanian. We look forward to seeing you in class!

Why Choose Us

Learn On Campus or From Home

We know students are fitting these courses into an already busy life! Everyone has their own learning style. Whether you want to learn from home or take your courses on our beautiful campus, we have you covered! At AFL, we make sure you have every resource available to make learning a new language as smooth and easy as possible.

Learn Quickly and Efficiently

Our cirriculum will have you speaking in your chosen language quickly! We have designed our courses so that you will be able to have engaging conversations after just a few lessons. We strive to make the learning process fun so you will even enjoy memorizing those sight words! The Academy of Foreign Language has assembled a team of the best instructors available!
